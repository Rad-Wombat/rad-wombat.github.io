Fun In A Box

Card game for 2-6 players ages 8 and up.

Authentic Fusion

Combines the best bits of trivia and light physical activities.

Family Friendly

Able to accommodate players of different ages and physical abilities in the same session.

How To Play

1. Everyone starts with a hand of challenge cards. Be the first to empty your hand!

2. During your turn, play a card to cover another card in the middle or double down for a challenge!

3. Attempt all shown cards within 30 seconds. It's all or nothing!

Preview rulebook or watch the How To Play video

About The Game

We have been extensively testing and refining the game over the year and launched a mini Kickstarter to get even more playtesters.

We're currently expanding the contents of the base game.

Rad Wombat Games

Rad Wombat Games is founded by Ryan and Ahmad. Anything You Can Do is our first co-design project.

Ryan Cross: I enjoy designing games that are innovative yet simple. My games have been showcased at PAX, SXSW, Boardgame BBQ, Game Expo and more. I've also won the From Scratch To Shelf design award.

Ahmad Salahuddin: I focus on creating thematic games. My first game Farmhouse Frenzy was featured at PAX 2023. My second game Silverbeard's Successor was published by the Murmuring Mystic as a limited physical release in Feb 2024.
